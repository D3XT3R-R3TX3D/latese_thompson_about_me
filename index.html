About Me!

Born and raised in East Orange, New Jersey, Latese Thompson carries a strong work ethic and an entrepreneurial spirit that has shaped her journey. From a young age, she was drawn to both business and creativity, always looking for ways to expand her knowledge and skills. Her natural drive and curiosity have led her to explore multiple industries, always seeking opportunities for growth. Professionally, Latese is a seasoned CPA and businesswoman with a deep understanding of finance and strategic management. She first made her mark in the entrepreneurial space as a sneaker reseller, navigating the fast-paced world of high-demand fashion. Now, she is channeling her expertise into launching a nonprofit trading foundation class, aimed at providing financial education and investment strategies to empower others in building long-term wealth.

Beyond her professional pursuits, Latese has a deep love for her godsons, often bonding with them over their shared passion for video games. As a lifelong gaming enthusiast, she appreciates the artistry and immersive storytelling of titles like Assassin’s Creed, Final Fantasy, Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege and The Division 2, and Spider-Man 1 & 2. She also enjoys unwinding at cigar lounges, where she engages in thoughtful conversations and relaxes after a busy day. And like her favorite cartoon cat, Garfield, she can never resist a good plate of lasagna.

Now, as she expands her expertise, Latese is delving into web development, recognizing the power of digital tools in today’s evolving business landscape. By mastering web technologies, she aims to enhance her business ventures, create engaging online platforms, and become a well-rounded, multi-faceted entrepreneur. Whether in finance, technology, or mentorship, Latese is committed to making a lasting impact, proving that adaptability and continuous learning are the keys to long-term success.
